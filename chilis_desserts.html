Rating Desserts From Chili's

Ah, Chili's. Known across the world for its Triple Dipper and $5 margaritas, this restaurant has made quite the name for itself since being founded in 1975. However, I'm not here to discuss its savory food or tasty drinks today. Instead, I want to focus on the establishment's limited line of desserts. Let's begin with perhaps the best treat Chili's has to offer: The chocolate chip cookie skillet.

Chocolate Chip Cookie Skillet - ★★★★☆

Via @chilisphilippines on Instagram

Baked and served in a cast iron skillet, this warm, gooey chocolate chip cookie is a fan favorite. Although its buttery base is good on its own, the firm vanilla ice cream that's strategically placed atop it takes this dessert to the next level. Who knew two sweets could balance each other out so well?

It's worth noting that the cookie skillet is best enjoyed when shared with one or more individuals. Otherwise, you'll start to understand why I rated it 4 out of 5 stars.

So, why is Chili's cookie skillet missing whole 1 star, according to my unprofessional rating? Well, as I mentioned above, it's not a very single-person friendly dessert. In other words, it's hard to eat alone. About halfway through consuming one of these bad boys, the chocolate taste becomes so overwhelming to the point where even the vanilla ice cream can't save the experience. Nevertheless, you try to make the cookie/frozen treat combination work. You do this in vain, of course, as you'll inevitably eat all the ice cream and still have something like a third of the cookie left, which by then you won't even want to look at. Thus, a 4-star rating is warranted for this dish.
